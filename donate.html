How Your Donation Helps

Cost of Feeding 40 Children

Your support helps us provide nutritious meals to 40 children at our centre. Below is a breakdown of the cost of preparing a full meal, as well as healthy add-ons that supplement the children's nutrition.

Full Meals

  • Ugali & Cabbage — 620 KES (≈ $5)
  • Rice & Beans — 1,500 KES (≈ $11)
  • Chapati & Beans — 1,800 KES (≈ $14)

Supplemental Nutrition

These additions help provide balanced nutrition alongside the main meals.

  • Fresh Fruit (Bananas + Oranges) — 400 KES (≈ $3)
  • Porridge — 420 KES (≈ $3)

A donation of 1,000 KES (≈ $8) can provide a nutritious full meal or help supply vital supplements such as fruit or porridge for the children.
