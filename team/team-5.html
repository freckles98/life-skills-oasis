Martin (James) Kamande

Assistant Social Worker

Martin is a compassionate and dedicated volunteer caregiver at Life Skills Oasis Centre, bringing over a year of hands-on experience. Since May 2024, he provides compassionate care to individuals, assisting with daily activities and giving them comfort. He is focused on fostering a positive and supportive environment and is eager to contribute to community development initiatives.

Martin is known for his strong leadership abilities, teamwork, and result-oriented approach. His core skills include Caregiving & support (ensuring comfort and safety) and Leadership & team coordination (leading volunteer teams to achieve organizational goals). In his role, he collaborates with volunteers to organize community outreach programs and has demonstrated leadership by coordinating volunteer schedules.

Additionally, his skills include Communication & rapport building (establishing trust with clients and team members) and Problem-solving and adaptability (addressing challenges promptly and adapting to changing situations).
