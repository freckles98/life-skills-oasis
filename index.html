Kenyan community based charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Life skills oasis is a unique community based organization providing holistic services to street and other acutely vulnerable children in the slums of Kiandutu, Thika, Kenya.
Holistic empowerment for the disadvantaged in the community.
A morally, socially, educationally and economically empowered community with improved living standards.

Programs Making a Difference

Rescue and Rehabilitation

Helping vulnerable children return home, return to school, and thrive.

Sports

Through football, we keep children active, involved, and on a positive path.

Feeding Program

We feed children daily, helping them stay focused, strong, and healthy.

Education Empowerment

Empowering disadvantaged youth through education and training.

Supporting parents, building brighter futures.

Helping the blind access resources and overcome challenges.
